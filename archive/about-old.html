About Us

Developing highly-integrated biosensor and system products for a new generation of point-of-care diagnostics systems

Our Mission

Anitoa Systems is developing highly-integrated biosensor and system products for a new generation of point-of-care diagnostics systems, targeting infectious diseases and other related conditions. Our current focus is a CMOS bio-optical molecular sensor that complements microfluidics to enable compact, field-portable nucleic-acid-test (NAT) and immunoassay platforms.

Our mission is to provide doctors and physicians a solution to perform rapid, yet precise diagnostics at point-of-care, instead of waiting for days and weeks to get the results from a centralized lab. Thus they can respond quickly with life-saving treatments, such as targeted antibiotics; and slow the spread of drug-resistant bacteria and viruses.

Team and partners

Our team consists of engineers and scientists with experiences in electronics, software, optics, biochemistry and clinical diagnostics. Many of us had worked and played leadership roles in well-known Silicon Valley IT and biotech companies such as ST Micro, NXP, Google, Tesla, Agilent, Chiron and Novartis.

We have also established partnerships with Zhejiang Nanosystems Institute (ZCNI) and the First Affiliated Hospital of Zhejiang University, P.R.China.
