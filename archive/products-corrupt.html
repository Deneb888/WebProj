Maverick, an Ultra-Portable qPCR System

Meet Maverick, an ultra-portable, fast, and affordable real time PCR system (qPCR) that is compatible with all standard qPCR and RT-qPCR assays. Target applications are rapid, on-the-site nucleic acid test of infectious diseases; blood screening; food safety and environmental monitoring.

Maverick features a 4-channel multiplex fluorescence optical system based on Anitoa CMOS ULS24 Ultra-low-light biosensor chip. With no internal moving parts, Maverick is rugged and requires no calibration to operate.

 

Maverick series of qPCR instruments include 4, 8, and 16 well models. All Maverick qPCR models are CE-IVD certified.

2019 New Coronavirus (COVID-19) Rapid Test Kit

(Research Use Only)

The COVID-19 Rapid Test Kit combines a Maverick ultra-portable Real Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (qPCR) instrument, with a “one-step” reverse transcription qPCR (RT-qPCR) reagent for testing COVID-19. This turn-key solution eliminates the cumbersome process of package and transporting patient samples to a central lab and the additional steps of tedious sample-prep prior to running RT-PCR. Anitoa COVID-19 and COVID-19/Influenza kits are for rapid screening only, not clinical confirmatory test.

Features

  • Completely self-contained turn-key solution for on-site testing.
  • Portable and quick time to results. ~35 min test time
  • Easy to operate one-step RT-PCR reagent. Separate DNA/RNA extraction NOT needed.
  • 4-channel multiplex assay to detect multi gene targets (ORF1ab, N and S (Spike) genes and an internal control) in one sample.

CMOS Ultra-low-light Bio-optical Sensor ULS24

(Now in volume production)

The Anitoa ULS24 is an ultra-low-light CMOS image sensor (CIS). Its low cost, small form factor and high level of integration make it optimally suited for use in a portable device in medical, scientific and industrial applications. An example of such application is a field portable nucleic-acid-test (NAT) system that performs molecular sensing based-on fluorescence or chemiluminescence signaling principles.

Features

  • Ultra low-light sensitivity. Detection threshold 3.0 x 10-6 lux
  • Low dark current; high SnR and wide dynamic range.
  • Built-in 12-bit ADCs; excellent linearity.
  • 3.3V and 1.8V power supply; 30mW max power.
  • Built-in temperature sensor; wide operating temperature range.
