What is MaverickCare?

When you face challenges while trying to meet a dealine, you need help right away. MaverickCare provides a completely new service experience. You receive proactive support through a single point of contact for instruments, software, and solutions. Get faster response times, faster access to specialized experts, and faster time to resolution.


MaverickCare is included with every new instrument purchase. With MaverickCare, you have access to Anitoa’s experts on every subject, as well as a portal of many of technical papers and application notes, so you can focus your own research and development with confidence.

MaverickCare with Standard Warranty

Each Maverick product is supplied with a one year standard warranty that covers all aspects of the use of the Maverick for mission-critical tasks such as diagnostics during an epidemic. Anitoa has staffed enough people and stocked with sufficient parts to ensure smooth operations of all the customer's Maverick products in the field.

  • 1-business-day technical response
  • Online knowledge center
  • Proactive software and firmware notifications
  • Warranty repair coverage - all parts and labor covered
  • 20 to 30-day instrument repair or replacement depending on nature of technical issue and location of customer.
